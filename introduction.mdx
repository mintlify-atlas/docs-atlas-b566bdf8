## What is Rotations?

Rotations is a unique music player that transforms how you experience your music collection. Unlike traditional players, Rotations automatically builds a dynamic rotation based on what you actually listen to, creating an ever-evolving soundtrack tailored to your preferences.

## How rotations work

Rotations gives you better control over your daily music experience:

- **Current Rotation**: Songs you listen to automatically enter your rotation
- **Rotation Radius**: Control how focused or diverse your listening experience is
- **Recommendations**: Get suggested songs outside your rotation to discover new music
- **Multi-rotation Mode**: Manage multiple rotations for different moods or contexts

<Note>
  Rotations is an import-only music player. You bring your own music from supported sources.
</Note>

## Built from first principles

Rotations reimagines the music listening experience by focusing on what matters: the music you actually want to hear, when you want to hear it. Instead of algorithms optimizing for engagement, Rotations puts you in control with a simple, intuitive rotation system.
